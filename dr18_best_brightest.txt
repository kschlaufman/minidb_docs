Columns
-------

designation - AllWISE source designation
ra_1 - All right ascension (J2000)
dec_1 - AllWISE declination (J2000)
glon - AllWISE galactic longitude

w1mpro - AllWISE W1 instrumental profile-fit photometry magnitude
w2mpro - AllWISE W2 instrumental profile-fit photometry magnitude
w3mpro - AllWISE W3 instrumental profile-fit photometry magnitude
w4mpro - AllWISE W4 instrumental profile-fit photometry magnitude
pmra - AllWISE apparent motion in ra
pmdec - AllWISE apparent motion in dec
j_m_2mass - J magnitude entry of the associated 2MASS All-Sky PSC source
h_m_2mass - H magnitude entry of the associated 2MASS All-Sky PSC source
k_m_2mass - K magnitude entry of the associated 2MASS All-Sky PSC source
ra_2 - APASS DR9 right ascension (J2000)
raerr - APASS DR9 right ascension uncertainty
dec_2 - APASS DR9 declination (J2000)
decerr - APASS DR9 declination uncertainty
nobs - APASS DR9 number of observed nights
mobs - APASS DR9 number of images for this field, usually nobs*5
vjmag - APASS DR9 Johnson V-band magnitude
bjmag - APASS DR9 Johnson B-band magnitude
gmag - APASS DR9 g'-band AB magnitude, Sloan filter
rmag - APASS DR9 r'-band AB magnitude, Sloan filter
imag - APASS DR9 i'-band AB magnitude, Sloan filter
evjmag - APASS DR9 Vmag uncertainty
ebjmag - APASS DR9 Bmag uncertainty
egmag - APASS DR9 g'mag uncertainty
ermag - APASS DR9 r'mag uncertainty
eimag - APASS DR9 i'mag uncertainty
name - APASS DR9 field name
separation - separation in arcseconds between AllWISE/APASS DR9 equitoiral coordinates
ebv - reddening predicted by SFD dust map
version - version 2 is high priority, version 1 is low priority (see Schlaufman & Casey 2014 for definitions)
