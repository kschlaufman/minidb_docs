Summary
-------




Columns
-------

source_id - Gaia DR2 source id
twomass - 2MASS ID
ra - Right ascention in J2000 reference frame
dec - Declination in J2000 reference frame
parallax - Parallax from Gaia DR2
id - Identification of a parent group from Kounkel et al. (2020)
g - Gaia (DR2) G band magnitude
bp - Gaia (DR2) BP band magnitude
rp - Gaia (DR2) RP band magnitude
j - 2MASS J band magnitude
h - 2MASS H band magnitude
k - 2MASS K band magnitude
age - Estimate of the age of the parent group from Kounkel et al. (2020)
eage - Uncertainty in age
av - Estimate of extinction of the parent group from Kounkel et al. (2020)
eav - Uncertainty in av
dist - Estimate of distance of the parent group from Kounkel et al. (2020)
edist - Uncertainty in dist
